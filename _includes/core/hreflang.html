{%- comment -%} Component: hreflang Path: _includes/core/hreflang.html Purpose: Emit tags for pages that have machine-generated translations, so search engines associate the English original with its /fr/ (etc.) alternates. Params: none — reads page.lang / page.translation_source_url and the generated _data/i18n/manifest.yml (scripts/translate.rb).
