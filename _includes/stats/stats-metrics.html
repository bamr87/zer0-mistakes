Statistics Help
About These Statistics

This dashboard displays comprehensive analytics about your site's content, including posts, categories, and tags.

Data Sources
  • Posts: All published content from your site
  • Categories: Content groupings and classifications
  • Tags: Topic labels and keywords
Updating Statistics

To refresh the data, run the statistics generator:

ruby _data/generate_statistics.rb
Data Freshness

Statistics are generated manually and should be updated regularly to reflect the latest content changes.
