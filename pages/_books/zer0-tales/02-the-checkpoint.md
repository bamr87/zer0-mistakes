On the far side of the tallest heading stood a little checkpoint hut, its windows glowing with friendly UI runes.

Zer0 knocked. The door opened all by itself, the way doors do in well-tested code.

{% include components/book-plate.html src="/assets/images/zer0-checkpoint-1.png" alt="A cozy mountain checkpoint with glowing runes where Zer0 raises a lantern" %}

Inside, warming itself by a small rendering fire, sat the missing pixel — bright as a firefly and a little embarrassed.

"I only wanted to see the view from the hero image," it blinked.

Zer0 smiled and held out a hand. "Then let's put you somewhere with a view."

And that is why, if you look very closely at the top of the home page, the very last pixel in the corner glows just a tiny bit warmer than the rest.

The site launched in the morning, with every pixel counted and not a single mistake.

Well — *zer0* mistakes, to be exact.
